about company

Recycling Army

Greest is an electronic waste recycling company based in Egypt and serving the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region. We are the first company in the Arab world offering green recycling of electronic waste and safe data destruction services. Our slogan is “Recycling Army”: when we partner with you, we become the recycling army that saves the earth from pollution and conserves its natural resources.
